चलका — To Splash · To Leap

UNC Chalkaa is a co-ed South Asian fusion dance team at UNC Chapel Hill. We blend Bharatanatyam, Bhangra, Hip Hop, Bollywood, and more — competing nationally, performing locally, and building community across North Carolina.

Who We Are

More than a dance team.

Started in 2008, Chalkaa has been a home at UNC for dancers who love South Asian culture and want to share it. We're a co-ed team — no experience required to audition — and we're student-run from top to bottom.

We take the name seriously: "Chalkaa" in Hindi means to splash or leap. That energy shows up in every performance — expressive, committed, and a little unpredictable in the best way.

Our Mission

Foster a deeper understanding of South Asian heritage in the Chapel Hill area, combining eastern and western dance styles to tell stories from popular culture and engage audiences of all ages.

What Drives Us

Three things we care about.

Every rehearsal, every competition, every community event comes back to these.

01
🪷
Cultural Representation

South Asian culture deserves a stage at UNC and beyond. We use dance as a way to share heritage with audiences who may never have seen Bharatanatyam or Bhangra performed live — and that matters.

02
🏅
Competitive Excellence

We show up to competitions like Legends and KKJ ready to compete with the best teams in the country. Months of work — choreography, formations, music, costuming — all build to those few minutes on stage.

03
🤲
Community Engagement

We participate in charity events, cultural festivals, and campus showcases. We run free workshops open to the public. We think about who we're dancing for — not just who's judging us.

Community Impact

Beyond the stage.

Competing is a big part of what we do, but it's not the whole picture. Chalkaa has always been about giving back to Chapel Hill and the broader North Carolina community.

  • 🎓
    Free Campus Workshops
    We host open Bollywood and fusion dance workshops for UNC students — free, no experience needed, just come out and learn something new.
  • 🌍
    Cultural Festivals & Showcases
    We perform at multicultural events across campus and in the broader Triangle area, bringing South Asian dance traditions to new audiences.
  • 💚
    Intentional Wellness Culture
    We take mental and physical health seriously — practices include snacks, recovery supplies, and open conversations about wellbeing. Dance teams can be high-pressure; we try to do it differently.
  • 🎪
    Charity & Local Events
    We've participated in charity events and community performances across the Research Triangle, using our platform to support causes that matter to our members.

Where We Compete

Representing UNC nationally.

We compete at some of the most competitive South Asian fusion events on the East Coast. These competitions draw teams from across the country — and we show up ready.

Legends
National
📍 National multi-city circuit
🏆 Top collegiate SA fusion competition

Our primary season goal and the main driver of our fundraising each year. Getting to Legends takes real resources — travel, costumes, registration — which is why community support matters so much.

KKJ
National
📍 East Coast collegiate circuit
🏆 High-caliber artistic competition

One of the most well-regarded SA fusion competitions in the country, drawing strong teams and serious judging panels. A major part of our competitive season.

UGA India Night
Regional
📍 University of Georgia
🎭 Cultural showcase & competition

A beloved regional event that brings together South Asian dance teams from across the Southeast. Great performances, great community — and a chance to connect beyond the UNC bubble.

Grant & Funding Appeal

Why we're worth supporting.

For organizations looking to invest in student arts, cultural programming, or community enrichment — here's the case for Chalkaa.

  • 01
    17 years of consistent operation
    Chalkaa has been active since 2008 — an unusually long run for a student org. That kind of longevity signals institutional culture, not just individual enthusiasm.
  • 02
    Tangible cultural education impact
    We bring South Asian dance forms — Bharatanatyam, Bhangra, Bollywood fusion — to audiences who've never encountered them. That's real cross-cultural education, live and in person.
  • 03
    Student leadership at every level
    Everything — from choreography to budgeting to sponsorship outreach — is student-run. Members develop skills in project management, finance, event production, and community organizing.
  • 04
    Low barriers, high access
    We actively keep participation affordable and welcoming. No experience required to audition. Free public workshops. A team culture designed to support — not pressure — its members.
  • 05
    Measurable reach and visibility
    Our competitions draw 10,000+ in-person attendees and 50,000+ online viewers. Our social channels and member networks extend that reach further with every post.
